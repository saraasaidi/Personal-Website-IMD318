✨ My Experience ✨

Internship

I completed my internship at Perpustakaan Negara Malaysia (PNM), where I learned about digital archiving, cataloging, and how information systems support public access. During the internship, I traveled to PNM by MRT. I also experienced what it is like to work in a real-life setting, and it was truly interesting.

UMBC

I participated in UMBC in Semester 2, where I gained valuable experiences, learned more about music, and discovered the challenges of synchronizing with a band. It was a worthwhile experience, even though I had to sacrifice my Semester 2 break. "A dream doesn't become reality through magic; it takes sweat, determination and hard work." – Colin Powell.

Exploring

Since starting my diploma at UiTM Machang, I have explored many beautiful places in Kelantan, including Buddhist temples like Wat Machimmaram and Wat Phothivihan, as well as unique mosques such as Masjid Ar-Rahman, Masjid Tengku Razaleigh, and Masjid Andalusia. These experiences have made my time on the East Coast (Kelantan, Terengganu, and Pahang) very memorable and enjoyable.

Driving

One of my exhilarating experiences was driving from Selangor to Kelantan for my studies. My first time driving all the way to UiTM Machang was in Semester 3. I felt really excited because it was my first long-distance drive without my family. It was so fun to drive fast and even overtake lorries and cars along the way—it felt like a real adventure! Moreover, I can go to anywhere I want.

Flood

It was my first time experiencing a flood during Semester 3. I had just returned from mid-semester break, and it had been raining non-stop that week. One morning, around 7 a.m., the water started rising, and I panicked a bit. I had to quickly pack my things and move my car into UiTM for safety. It was definitely a thrilling and unforgettable experience.
